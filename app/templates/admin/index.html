Instructions

  • Step 1: Create a new "make" from the Configuration heading. For instance, this could be Budweiser or Trillium.
  • Step 2: Create a new "model" from the Configuration heading. For instance, this could be Julius or Melcher Street.
    • This list of models is what forms the collection of surveys that is available to non-admin users.
  • Step 3: Run a survey. Use the Log to see the survey completion percentage.
    • You do not need to do anything in the app to "run a survey". Simply have your users log in and submit their rating.
  • Step 4: Review the results with the Results view.
    • Click on an ID number for additional detail.
Cleaning the Database

To clean the database before running a series of surveys selecect Advanced > Clean the database. This will delete all non-admin users and survey entries from the database. It will not delete makes and models.

Clearing the Database

If you wish to start fresh you can clear the database entirely. This will delete everything except for the 'admin' user.
